Common Questions

Do I need to be tech-savvy to use RouteBoost? +
Not at all. RouteBoost is built specifically for busy food truck owners. The interface is mobile-first, uses large buttons, minimal typing, and plain-language AI. If you can use Instagram, you can use RouteBoost.
Does RouteBoost replace Square or Toast? +
No. RouteBoost is not a point-of-sale or payment system. It sits on top of your existing tools and focuses on what they don't — growing your customer base through better locations, marketing, and event bookings.
What happens after my 7-day free trial? +
After your trial, RouteBoost is $25/month. No credit card is required to start the trial. You can cancel anytime — no contracts, no hassle.
Will AI post to my social media automatically? +
In the current version, RouteBoost generates your post and you review it before sharing. You always have final approval. Fully automated posting is on the roadmap for a future update.
How do I cancel my subscription? +
You can cancel anytime from your profile settings inside the app, or by emailing support@routeboost.com. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.
