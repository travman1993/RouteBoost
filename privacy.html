Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

RouteBoost ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of food truck owners and operators who use our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the RouteBoost application and website.

In short: we collect only what we need to make RouteBoost work for you, we never sell your data, and you can delete your account and data at any time.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information — When you sign up, we collect your email address, password (stored securely as a hash, never in plain text), and your food truck name.

Truck Profile Data — Information you provide about your food truck, including cuisine type, menu descriptions, operating hours, and branding details. This is used to generate AI content and personalize your experience.

Location Data — Weekly route locations you add (addresses, schedules, notes). We also collect daily check-in confirmations. We do not track your GPS location in the background.

Feedback Data — End-of-day ratings, notes, and factors you provide (weather, crowd levels, etc.). This is used to improve AI predictions for your truck specifically.

Usage Data — How you interact with the app (screens visited, features used, post generation frequency). This helps us improve the product.

Payment Information — Subscription payments are processed by a third-party payment processor. We do not store your credit card number, expiration date, or CVV on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Generate AI-powered daily plans, social media posts, and event application messages
  • Provide personalized location recommendations and demand predictions
  • Deliver AI growth insights based on your feedback history
  • Surface relevant event and booking opportunities in your area
  • Improve the accuracy of our AI models over time
  • Send essential account communications (verification, billing, security)
  • Maintain and improve the RouteBoost platform

3. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • We do not share your individual truck performance data with other users
  • We do not track your GPS location in the background
  • We do not send marketing emails without your consent
  • We do not store your payment card details on our servers

4. AI-Generated Content

RouteBoost uses artificial intelligence to generate social media posts, event applications, and growth insights. These are generated based on your truck profile and activity data. You always have the ability to review, edit, or discard AI-generated content before it is used or shared.

AI processing occurs only when triggered by your actions (generating a post, requesting suggestions, etc.). There is no continuous background AI monitoring.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure, encrypted servers. We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), hashed passwords, and access controls. While no system is 100% secure, we take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

6. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to operate RouteBoost, including:

  • Payment processing — to handle subscription billing securely
  • AI providers — to generate content and insights (your data sent to AI providers is used only for generating your content and is not used to train their models)
  • Hosting and infrastructure — to serve the application reliably
  • Analytics — to understand how users interact with the product (anonymized and aggregated)

These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription, your data is retained for 30 days in case you reactivate. After 30 days, or upon your request, your data is permanently deleted from our servers.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correct your data — update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your data — request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your data — receive your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@routeboost.com.

9. Cookies

RouteBoost uses essential cookies to keep you logged in and maintain your session. We may use analytics cookies to understand usage patterns. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies from third parties.

10. Children's Privacy

RouteBoost is designed for business use by food truck owners and operators. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you via email or through the app. Continued use of RouteBoost after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled:

Email: privacy@routeboost.com
